About Me

Thank you so much for taking an interest in who I am and what I represent.

As a son of immigrants, I've seen first hand just how hard folks opening their first businesses have it. My family was no different. When we started their cleaning business Home Sweet Home Cleaners, without any meaningful online presence, we struggled to find clients. There was so much that our business lacked in order to grow at a healthy pace and remain competitive. For quite some time we struggled. Luckily, a software engineer client presented us with a fully functional site as a Christmas present! How thoughtful is that?! Witnessing first hand just how much this changed our business; helping my parents accomplish their goals and dreams. I knew that this was something valuable and I kept it close to my heart.

After finishing college with the goal of entering Medical School; life took an unexpected turn and I redirected my efforts towards helping my loved ones. In the aftermath, I resumed my passion for helping folks in a different way. I am on a mission in creating websites and web applications to empower businesses and individuals in my community. As a software engineer developing fullstack websites and web applications, I've helped folks take the necessary steps to achieve their goals and their dreams. I look forward to working with you in developing world class solutions for whatever challenges the future may bring.

Reach on out! Let's start a conversation and see how I can best help you.
